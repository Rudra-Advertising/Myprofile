Campaign Case Study: NerdWallet

Payday & Consumer Loan Promotions – Budget: $780

Campaign Overview

NerdWallet initiated a high-intent lead generation campaign focused on Payday and Consumer Loans targeting users across the United States. With a total ad spend of $780, we used a mixed pricing strategy incorporating both CPC (Cost Per Click) and CPL (Cost Per Lead) to drive traffic and quality lead submissions.

Execution Highlights

  • Custom landing pages focused on quick loan approvals and credit support.
  • Mobile-first design with click-to-apply CTA buttons.
  • Time-sensitive ads for weekdays with high demand around salary dates.
  • Keyword targeting: “fast payday loans,” “same-day loans,” and “low interest personal loans.”

Conclusion

The campaign achieved consistent daily traffic through CPC while CPL led to higher-quality leads with a conversion uplift. Overall, the targeted ad strategy and adaptive landing page funnel generated a significant ROI and positioned NerdWallet as a go-to resource for urgent and personalized loan solutions.
