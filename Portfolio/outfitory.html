Campaign Overview

Outfitory Ltd. launched a campaign to generate quality leads and increase apparel product sales in USA and India. The total spend was $500, and the pricing strategy included CPL (Cost Per Lead) and CPS (Cost Per Sale) modules for performance marketing.

Campaign Strategy

  • Seasonal collection ads targeting fashion-conscious buyers.
  • Lead magnets with discount sign-up offers for CPL.
  • Product carousel ads on social platforms and Google Shopping.
  • Smart bidding optimization for high-value purchases.
