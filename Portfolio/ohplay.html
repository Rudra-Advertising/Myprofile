Campaign Case Study: Ohplay.club

Multi-Geo Performance Marketing – Budget: $1200

Campaign Overview

Ohplay.club launched a multi-geo advertising campaign targeting Canada, USA, Germany, and India. The total allocated budget was $1200. We employed a dual pricing model with CPC (Cost Per Click) and CPA (Cost Per Acquisition) strategies to maximize ROI and reach specific audience segments in each region.

Strategy & Execution

  • Localized ad creatives based on user behavior in each geo.
  • Segmentation by device type and traffic quality.
  • Integrated crypto-oriented CTA: “Deposit in Dollars, Cash Out in Doge.”
  • Day-parting for USA and Canada to improve CPA results.

Conclusion

The campaign delivered high engagement in India and USA, with CPC driving the most traffic while CPA provided higher-quality leads. Through aggressive optimization and geo-targeting, Ohplay.club achieved a strong ROI and increased user retention through crypto incentives.
