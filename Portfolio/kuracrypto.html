Campaign Case Study: Kuracrypto.club

Crypto Awareness & Engagement – Budget: $2500

Campaign Overview

Kuracrypto.club executed a performance-driven campaign to boost awareness and interest around their new crypto asset in Japan. The total budget of $2500 was spent using Cost Per Click (CPC) and Cost Per Mile (CPM) pricing models to increase visibility and engagement.

Strategy and Targeting

  • Crypto-focused content placements on Japanese tech blogs and financial sites.
  • Programmatic display banners for desktop and mobile users.
  • Localized CTA: “Join Japan’s Fastest Growing Coin.”
  • Banner A/B testing across CPM and CPC models.

Conclusion

Kuracrypto.club successfully expanded its footprint in the Japanese crypto space. The CPM model was effective in building broad visibility while CPC ensured deeper user interaction. The campaign performance demonstrated a well-balanced funnel between awareness and conversion, setting the stage for Kuracrypto’s next token launch phase.
