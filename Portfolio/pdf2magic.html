Campaign Case Study: PDF2Magic.com

Utility Tool Promotion – Budget: $150

Campaign Overview

PDF2Magic.com, a global PDF editing and conversion tool, ran a targeted campaign with a $150 budget on a CPC (Cost Per Click) model. The goal was to increase global traffic and onboard freemium users through utility-related search and contextual ads.

Execution Strategy

  • Keyword targeting: “free PDF converter”, “edit PDF online”, “merge PDF tool”.
  • Ad distribution via Google Search and Display Network.
  • English-only campaigns targeting all devices globally.
  • Call-to-action: “Convert PDFs Instantly – No Sign-Up Required”.

Top Traffic Regions

Daily Click Growth

Conclusion

Despite a small spend, PDF2Magic.com effectively reached users in need of a simple online PDF solution. The campaign recorded an increasing CTR trend across the week, with India, the US, and Philippines showing high interaction rates. CPC optimization kept costs minimal and conversion rates healthy.
