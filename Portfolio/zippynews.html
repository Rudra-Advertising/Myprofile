Campaign Overview

Zippy News ran a promotional campaign targeting news readers in USA and India using a combination of CPC and CPM models. With a modest budget of $350, the focus was on maximizing click-through and building impressions in top-tier content regions.

Strategy & Highlights

  • Headline-focused native banners to attract mobile traffic.
  • Time-of-day segmentation for morning and evening peak readership.
  • Used Google and Taboola inventory for expanded reach.
  • Multiple creatives rotated for freshness in both markets.

Conclusion
